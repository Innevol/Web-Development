This is the main website where you can book your stay to a hotel in Hawaii. You are
given the ability to research Hawaii's main website from the information link
provided in the navigation bar. You can choose between three different rooms
ranging from single, double, queen, and king sizes. One person has the ability to
order up to 3 rooms, and must be 18 and older. When you enter your information
and hit confirm, you are sent to a confirmation page where you are given a unique
confirmation number and may review your information. Update and cancel options
are given to allow you to cancel your reservation, and update your information.
From there, you can sit back, relax, and pack for your vacation!
